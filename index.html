  • In the Resident Evil/Biohazard of 1996 there were many types of enemies, which had their on unic style and history, and in this website there are some information regarding a little about their original form as well as its transformations. All the creatures in the game (Except the zombie and the crows) were created as B.O.W.s (or Bio Organic Weapons) by a virus named T-virus (or Tyrant-Virus), devolopt by the Umbrella Corporation, that promoted mutations on the infected by it, and this is why they are so distinct from their original form. The game takes place in the Spencer Mansion which was a laboratory used to create some of the enemies.

  • Ahead there are some articles that shows how the enemies look as well as some information about them.

    • ZOMBIE (T-Virus)

      ZOMBIE (T-Virus)

      • The people that turn into the normal zombie variant were turned because of the side effect directly caused by the leaked t-virus, which make the infected shows loss of skin, loss of intellect and more durability in their bodies, which also make them some very resilient threat.

    CERBERUS

      CERBERUS

      • The Cerberus dogs were a project that aimed to test the T-Virus in dogs of doberman race, which make them more agressive and more durable, similar to the zombie.

    CROWS

      CROWS

      • The Crows that appear during the course of the game are simply normal crows that are more agressive than usual because of the T-Virus. The crows in the game were infected by eaten ifected flesh of dead bodies.

    YAWN

      YAWN

      • The Yawn is a giant Snake that originally was a normal snake (Adder Snake found in the surroundings of the Arkley Mountains) that have been infected by T-Virus, making it grow larger and stronger, also making its venom a lot stronger too.

    WEB SPINNERS

      WEB SPINNERS

      • The Web Spinners that are found in the Spencer Mansion were originally normal Spiders that were iffected with the T-Virus, making them bigger with a stronger venom like spit acid, however they dont have the capacity to produce spider web.

    BLACK TIGER

      BLACK TIGER

      • The Black Tiger is a giant Web Spinner that is six times bigger then the normal Web spinner and have capacity to produce web, making it more dangerous because of the size and the capability to capture its enemies with the web it produces.

    PLANT-42

      PLANT-42

      • The PLANT-42 is a monster like plant that grows and attacks uncontrollable, it attacks by hitting and spitting acid with its appendages. The PLANT-42 originally was project to infuse the T-Virus in to plants, however it got out of control because of its side effects. The other mutated plants in the Spencer Mansion were ifected by contaminated water.

    WASPS

      WASPS

      • The Wasps, like crows, are simply bigger wasps with an agressive behavior. They were infected by T-Virus in a numbers of manners. One of them was by feeding on the pollen of PLANT-42.

    NEPTUNE

      NEPTUNE

      • The Neptune project (code:FI-03), was a project to create subspecies of great-white sharks using T-Virus to create sometype bio-weaponry, which make them more agressive and sometimes bigger than the avarage great-white shark.

    HUNTER ALPHA

      HUNTER ALPHA

      • The Hunters (code of the project:MA-121) found in the Spencer Mansion were the first B.O.W to be fully-functional. Created by injecting repitilian DNA into human embryos with the T-Virus. It had very high intellect, speed and strength.

    CHIMERA

      CHIMERA

      • The Chimeras are human-fly hybrids, created, like the hunters, by injecting fly DNA into human embryos of homeless woman (forcibly impregnated). Its attacks consists in hurt its victum with their claws from different angles.

    TYRANT (T-002 MODEL)

      TYRANT (T-002 MODEL)

      • The Tyrant T-002 was created as the ultimate bio-weapon for replace soldiers in combat. It was Created by finding the best human genetic to suport the T-Virus without dying or transforming into conventional zombies, with its mutations providing a giant Claw in the left arm and an exposed heart. It has high durability, speed, strength , height (2,62M) and the same level of intellect of normal humans. In the game its the final boss, its strength is was high that could impale the player using its claws.
